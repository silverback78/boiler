StudyWidgets works with your notes.

  1. Type your notes in a way StudyWidgets understands it *
  2. Copy your notes
  3. Paste your notes into the editor

* OneNote (no affiliation) works best, and it's free. Windows 10 — Online

On the next page you'll see how to take notes so that StudyWidgets can turn them into instant study material.
