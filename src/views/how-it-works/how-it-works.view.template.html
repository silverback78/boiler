How it Works

Study

With StudyWidgets you simply create a deck and then choose how you want to study.

You can study the entire deck at once, flip through flashcards, or answer multiple choice questions.

Each widget has a progress indicator. When you get a card right, the progress moves forward. When you get a card wrong, it moves backwards. Be careful, the bar moves backwards more quickly than it moves forward.

When you get a card wrong, you will see that card more frequently in the current study session.

At the end of the study session, you will have the option to study only the cards you missed during that session.

When you get a card right, the progress moves forward.
When you miss a card, the progress moves backwards.
When you are done you can study only missed cards.

Create a Deck

StudyWidgets works with your notes. You create decks of cards by typing your notes, copying and then pasting into the new deck form.

You must use a format that StudyWidgets understands. The easiest is a format that works well with Microsoft OneNote (but can also be used in a text editor of your choice). Both Windows Store and Web versions of OneNote are free.

  1. Type your title, press enter
  2. Type the first term, press tab
  3. Type the definition, press enter

The results in OneNote create a table for you automatically. You can create multiple decks simultaneously, just separate decks by pressing enter.
